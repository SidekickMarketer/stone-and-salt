## PART 5: CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### What Will Make or Break Your Business

**1. Service Quality & Reliability**
- Show up when you say you will
- Do excellent work every time
- Communicate proactively with clients
- Handle problems professionally

**2. Financial Management**
- Track every dollar in and out
- Maintain cash flow buffer
- Price services profitably
- Invest in growth strategically

**3. Client Relationships**
- Build trust through consistent delivery
- Ask for referrals and reviews
- Stay in touch between services
- Upsell additional services naturally

**4. Marketing & Brand Presence**
- Professional online presence
- Active social media engagement
- Local SEO optimization
- Word-of-mouth cultivation

**5. Operational Systems**
- Efficient scheduling and routing
- Quality equipment maintenance
- Crew training and management
- Scalable processes and procedures

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

**❌ Underpricing Services**
- Don't compete on price alone
- Premium positioning requires premium pricing
- Calculate true costs including overhead

**❌ Overextending Too Fast**
- Grow capacity in line with demand
- Don't buy equipment before you need it
- Hire crew members as workload justifies

**❌ Neglecting Marketing**
- Consistent marketing is essential
- Online presence is non-negotiable
- Client acquisition is ongoing process

**❌ Poor Financial Management**
- Separate business and personal finances
- Track profitability by service line
- Maintain adequate insurance coverage

**❌ Sacrificing Quality for Volume**
- Reputation is everything in service business
- One bad client experience can cost 10 referrals
- Better to do less work excellently than more work poorly
